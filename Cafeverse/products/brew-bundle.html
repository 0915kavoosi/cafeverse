Product introduction

Brazilian Gold is one of the most well-known coffees from Brazil and is typically mild and balanced in flavor. It is known for its low acidity and medium body, with flavors often including notes of chocolate and roasted nuts. After drinking, it leaves a smooth, natural sweetness in the mouth, making it suitable for daily consumption.
